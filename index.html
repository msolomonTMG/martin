MY PHILOSOPHY OF LIFE

THE DISCIPLIN

It is the same journey like keeping you budy in shape or beeing in a sports team. That means of cuase you are an individual person and at he end of the day you are fighting for yourselfe and your life but especially in times where everybody is focused on his self-realization you always need to know your are working in a team. And a team is just working fine when every component is operates like a gearwheel. Simply if you are not having your own disciplin to get thinks done in a right way the system won't work.

THE FOCUS

Allways do what you love and are having a passion for. That is the only way to stay focused. And you need to stay focused in an environment that is always changing faster then you probably think about. This impact consists of influencing framworks who will constantly change your journey of success but keep always in mind to never lose the final destination including an option B and a possible exit.

THE NETWORK

You have to start early to grow a big and worth network. It doesn't make any sense to be focused on a quantitative network that doesn't help you out with any of your needs. Treat the people you know as friends and always stay in a good, warm and personal relationship with all of them. Remember that it will push you a lot to orientate yourself upwards but you also have the social duty to promote those who can cover your back.
